Bio

Just some trans faggottttt hi

I'm an artist and translator who loves technology, software, machines, and wires. My hobbies are webmastery (of course), playing video games, drawing, and listening to music. I'm not much of a writer, but I do love to ramble about the things I like.

I grew up playing on my dad's computer and my mom's Famicom and Game Boy, so it's only natural I'd ended up loving tech and video games! My childhood favorites were Link's Awakening, Kirby's Dream Land 2, and Super Mario Bros. 3.

Since I'm from a Japanese household, I also have been reading manga and watching anime since I was very, very young. I remember loving おしえて菜花 - Oshiete Nanoka, the Legend of Zelda manga by Himekawa Akira (especially Four Swords, the Minish Cap, and Majora's Mask), and Naruto. I want to make shrines for them (and more!) sometime in the future.

Also, we're actually a decently big plural system, but it's sort of whatever. It might become relevant sometime, it might not.
